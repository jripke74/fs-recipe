Creamy Chocolate Fudge

Description

A recipe for pulled pork that everyone will love.

A recipe for pulled pork that everyone will love.

Ingredients

  • Pulled pork shoulder
  • Fresh ground pepper and sea salt

Steps

  1. Trim access fat and slice fat
  2. Add pans of water to add moister to pork
  3. Cook on 210 or lower for 6 hours with smoke
  4. after 6 hours wrap in paper or foil
  5. cook on 210 or lower for at least another 8 hours
